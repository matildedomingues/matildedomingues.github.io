We are enough exactly as we are

Each mark on our body tells a part of our story. It can either be an unfortunate memory from our childhood, the result of an act of courage at some point, or simply a characteristic that sets us apart and may even have been born with us. To accept these characteristics is to accept our history as it is, without reproach. When we decide to love every part of ourselves, we are also redefining beauty to include authenticity and individuality. Therefore, we must look at all of this and not see it as imperfections, but rather recognise that it is what makes us unique.
