Echoes of Silence

"Echoes of Silence" shows that having friends around becomes not only a comfort, but also a vital necessity. In the midst of revolt, anguish and sadness, the presence of an attentive friend can be the pivotal point that can help us emerge from the darkness. Talking about our problems is often the first crucial step on the journey to recovery. The simple presence of someone who cares about us can rekindle the flame of hope and remind us that giving up isn't an option.
