Friendly Look

We live in a time when image is highly valued, essentially in relation to others. "Friendly Look" refers to a way of seeing someone with empathy, understanding and acceptance, focusing on the person's positive qualities and real value, rather than judging based on appearances and life's flaws. This look is characterized by kindness, support and a vision that seeks the well-being and happiness of the other.
