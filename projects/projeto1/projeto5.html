About yourself

It's not just an exhibition of physical and emotional scars, but a celebration of the courage that each person shows in facing and accepting their imperfections, promoting a dialog about self-acceptance and human resilience. This project consists of several short video testimonies in which their identities are concealed. Each testimonial aims to tell the story of their body's greatest weaknesses, the story behind those marks and whether or not they manage to cope with it all, explaining their acceptance processes.
